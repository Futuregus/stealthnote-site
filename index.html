What is StealthNote?

StealthNote is a lightweight, secure notepad application that encrypts your notes with strong encryption—perfect for storing passwords, confidential information, or sending secret messages safely.

✨ Features

  • 💾 Encrypted Save/Load – Secure your notes with password-based encryption.
  • 🌙 Themes – Choose from pre-made themes or make your own here.
  • 🔠 Adjustable Font – Set the font and size to feel your own.
  • 📂 Default Folder – Set your favorite folder as the starting point.
  • 🔧 Persistent Settings – Remembers your preferences every time you open it.
  • 🛡️ Password Strength Check – Warns you if your password is weak.
  • 🕵️ StealthMode – Quickly hides notes and opens the calculator app.
  • 🌐 Update Checker – Automatically checks GitHub for new versions.
  • 📖 Open source
